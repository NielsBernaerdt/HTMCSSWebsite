SquirrelMania!

C++ UE4 GroupsWork

Github Link:

Description:

In this course I got through the process used to build games and 3d applications.
The goal was to create a vertical slice of a game, while going through the complete production pipeline from concept to final product.
The following topics got covered: The global production process and the planning of a 3D application. | Design documents. | Version Control Systems. | Team and production manaagement.

Features:

- Parallax Camera:

A parallax effect consisting of 3 layers created through Unreal Shading & Materials.

-Double Jump:

A double jump, which resets after touching the sides/ceiling.

-Downwards air attack:

The player can use a downwards attack move while in the air.

- Charge attack:

Holding the attack stacks the damage and results in a different animation.
